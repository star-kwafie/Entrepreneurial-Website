"What is Penbook?" you ask?

We will now answer your question...

A penbook is a stylishly decorated notebook, with matching, dry-erase labels. The many patterns make it so that you will always find one for you.

You can use a penbook for:

  • Schoolwork! You can write in it! (No kidding. It's a notebook.)
  • Bragging to your friends about how pretty your brand new notebook is.
